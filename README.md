# DeadlineManager
[中文版](README_ZH.md)
## Introduction
DeadlineManager is an app designed to help users manage their deadlines effectively with AI assistance.The app is built on the Android platform. Users input their deadline details and specific dates, and the AI breaks down the tasks and generates a visual schedule to help users better plan and complete their tasks.

## Core Features
1. **Deadline Breakdown and Management** - The AI breaks down deadlines into smaller, more specific tasks, helping users better plan their time and reduce anxiety.
2. **Dynamic Task Status Updates** - Users can update the completion status of tasks in real-time, and the app will dynamically adjust the priority and time allocation of remaining tasks to ensure efficient completion.
3. **Intelligent Reminders and Personalized Suggestions** - Based on task priority and due dates, the app will intelligently remind users of upcoming deadlines. Additionally, it will provide personalized suggestions based on user habits and preferences.
4. **Visual Schedule** - The app generates a clear visual schedule to help users intuitively understand the distribution and timing of their tasks, thereby improving time management efficiency.

## Tech Stack
- Android Development: Kotlin
- AI Integration: **[KOOG](https://github.com/JetBrains/koog)** (Java version of the Langchain library)
